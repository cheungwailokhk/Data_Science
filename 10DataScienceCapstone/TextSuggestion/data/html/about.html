Application features:

  • Automatic suggestion
  • Build your own model
  • Generate "composition"
  • Interactive selection
  • Suggestion visualization

How to use:

  • You can type a text directly in the input box to search for suggestions for the next word. Suggested words are in the Wordcloud and the bar chart.
  • Clicking the word cloud and the bar chart can input a suggested word.
  • If you select the Auto-Suggestion, suggestions are automatically updated in the plots based on your input.
  • You can build your model by uploading a text file. Please make sure to upload a .txt file.
    (Example: example.txt)
  • In the building process, numbers, punctuations, English stopwords, any special characters, and non-English words are automatically removed.
  • After you built your model, you can select to use our default model or your prediction model.
  • In the "For Fun" tab, our application can generate an English-like composition based on your input.
